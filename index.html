Zagster Rides: 2016, 2017, 2018

This is a doughnut chart showing the number of rides per year at all Zagster stations. The data is being directly pulled and updated from the heroku app. Using the Chart.js library I am able to visualize the data directly from the database. It shows the overall number of rides from the years 2016-2018. As Zagster continues to grow, it will be easy to add new years with new data. The graph will have a greater effect as new data is entered.

The presented graph could be used if Zagster was trying to expand in Bend, potential investors would see the large growth that has happen within the past 3 years.
